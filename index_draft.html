I study a type of bone cancer called osteosarcoma that occurs in kids, teenagers and young adults. Confused? See The Fault in Our Stars!
Osteosarcoma worst form of bone cancer in youth and improvements in treatment for patients have remained stagnant for the past three decades especially for patients whose cancer travels to other places in their body, or metastasizes.
DNA is the language that your body uses to communicate. Epigenetics is a fancy word for things that affect your body but don't change the DNA.
Like a detective, I try to find clues that lead to identifying the 'culprits' that cause cancer and find way to take them out.
